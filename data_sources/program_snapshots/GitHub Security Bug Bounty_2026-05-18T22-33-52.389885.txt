
        Bug Bounty Program: https://bounty.github.com

        - *.example.com

        - marketing.example.com

        - Social engineering

        - Critical: $5,000 - $15,000

        - Medium: $500 - $2,500
        - Low: $100 - $500

        - Report findings within 24 hours
